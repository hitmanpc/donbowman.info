# 1993 Mustang Coyote Swap

Outside of professional software development, I work on a 1993 Mustang Foxbody with a Gen 2 Coyote engine and 6R80 automatic transmission.

This project combines mechanical work, electrical troubleshooting, diagnostics, tuning workflows, and software ideas.

## Why it matters

The project is personal, but it also reflects the same mindset I bring to software engineering: break down complex systems, test assumptions, isolate problems, and improve the result.

## Areas of work

- Coyote swap integration
- 6R80 transmission support
- Wiring and grounding diagnostics
- OBD2 and CAN data exploration
- PCMTEC tuning research
- Custom dashboard planning
- Real-world troubleshooting

## What this demonstrates

This project shows curiosity, persistence, systems thinking, and the ability to work across unfamiliar technical domains.
